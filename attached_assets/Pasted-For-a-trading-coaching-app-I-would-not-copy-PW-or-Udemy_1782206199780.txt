For a trading coaching app, I would **not copy PW or Udemy directly**, but I would take inspiration from their strengths:

* PW → clean education-focused UI, high readability
* Udemy → premium feel, strong content hierarchy

For a **Trading Education App**, users spend long periods watching charts, lectures, PDFs, and quizzes. The UI should feel professional, trustworthy, and premium.
